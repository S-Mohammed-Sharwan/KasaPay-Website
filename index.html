About KasaPay

KasaPay is a specialized payment platform designed specifically for waste collection teams in India. Our mission is to simplify and secure the Bill Payment process for both collection agents and consumers.

Operating primarily in Mangalore, Karnataka, we have successfully served over 20,000 users with our reliable and user-friendly payment solutions. Our platform bridges the gap between traditional waste collection methods and modern digital payment systems.
